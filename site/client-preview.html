About Jess

Fifteen years in arts, health & community

Jess Linton is an HCPC registered Art Psychotherapist, Visual Artist and Creative Practitioner. She has been working creatively in community, arts, social action, education and health settings with all ages for over 15 years.

She is an advocate for play and curiosity as a basic human right — for helping us to explore and make sense of ourselves and the world around us.
