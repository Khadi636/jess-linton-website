## Main Text

- Individual art therapy in a safe, private & confidential setting
- Short-term or long-term intervention
- Consultation & support to professionals (teachers, care workers, special educational needs co-ordinators, etc.) working with children, adults & families in need of emotional and psychological support
- Support with referrals to statutory and community services
- Clinical Supervision — with particular expertise in: children; young people; adult; supporting low to moderate anxiety; low mood and depression; experiences of trauma (where specialist intervention is not required); refugee and asylum seeking community; working creatively; establishing community projects and psycho-social spaces; inclusive arts; arts and health
- Skill sharing: facilitating conversation; workshops, seminars & lectures; exhibitions
