## Main Text

Creatively collaborating with a range of communities / groups / inspired individuals is a focus of all of Jess's work. Over the past decade and a half Jess has worked with: early years, families, children, young people, adults of all ages. She is experienced in working in a range of settings and contexts:

- **SCHOOLS:** working with curriculum links, out of school hours learning, summer courses, Arts Award
- **SPECIAL EDUCATIONAL NEEDS:** including artist training in working with children with mild to severe learning, mental and physical health difficulties
- **YOUTH GROUPS:** working with young people from design to delivery of arts projects which support them to celebrate their sense of agency
- **ADULT MENTAL HEALTH CENTRES:** long and short term artistic projects and skill sharing for adults in community mental health communities and centres, developed in collaboration with them and co-facilitating groups and workshops with service users
- **FAMILIES:** open workshops; longer-term family learning projects; creative family events
- **HOSPITALS:** Summer courses; Hospital schools (Chelsea & Westminster), designing bespoke and one off workshops (Guys & St Thomas, Barts); Adolescent Psychiatric Units (Coborn, East London NHS Foundation Trust)
- **REFUGEE NETWORKS:** one off workshops, youth projects, community networks, supporting design, delivery and offering emotional support and signposting for unique projects such as: Parwaz VR; Refugee Tales; The Paper Project, public sessions, training.
