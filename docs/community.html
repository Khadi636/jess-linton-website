Art as a force for connection

For over a decade, Jess has worked with community organisations, schools, NHS trusts and refugee networks to create participatory art experiences that are meaningful, joyful and genuinely transformative.

Her work recognises that creativity is not a luxury. It is a way of knowing ourselves, each other, and the world we share.
