Cookie Policy

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website stores in your browser when you visit. They help websites remember your preferences or enable certain functionality. This website also uses browser local storage, which works similarly but stores data in your browser rather than sending it with every request.

2. Cookies and storage we use

We use only a single item of local storage, described below. We do not set any cookies of our own.

Name Type Purpose Duration
jl_cookie_consent Local storage Remembers whether you have accepted or declined the cookie banner, so it is not shown on every page. Until you clear browser data

3. Third-party cookies and services

Some third-party services used by this site may set their own cookies or log data when you visit:

  • Google Fonts: We load typefaces from fonts.googleapis.com. Google may log your IP address. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages: This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log access data for security and reliability purposes. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.
  • Squarespace CDN: Some images on this site are served from images.squarespace-cdn.com. Squarespace may log access data when images are loaded. See Squarespace's Privacy Policy.

4. What we do not use

  • Analytics cookies or services (e.g. Google Analytics, Matomo)
  • Advertising or targeting cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Any cookie that tracks you across other websites

5. How to manage cookies and local storage

You can clear local storage and cookies at any time through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search and services → Clear browsing data

Clearing this data will reset your cookie consent preference and the banner will appear again on your next visit.

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please email jess.a.linton@gmail.com.
