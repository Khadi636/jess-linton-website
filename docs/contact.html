Therapy Referrals

Make a referral

Jess accepts referrals from professionals (social workers, GPs, teachers, family workers) as well as self-referrals from individuals and families.

To make a referral, please send a short email outlining the individual's situation. A full history is not required at this stage — Jess will respond to discuss suitability and next steps.

Please obtain consent from anyone you are referring before making contact. All communication is handled in strict confidence, in line with HCPC standards of professional conduct.
